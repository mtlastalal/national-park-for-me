About MTL National Park

Preserving Nature • Inspiring Generations • Connecting People with the Wild

Who We Are

Established in 1985, MTL National Park is a vast sanctuary dedicated to conserving wildlife, protecting rare ecosystems, and offering a space where people can reconnect with nature. Spanning over 50,000 acres of lush forests, crystal-clear lakes, and diverse habitats, our park is home to hundreds of plant and animal species. We are more than just a park — we are a living classroom, an outdoor adventure, and a safe haven for biodiversity. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to balance environmental protection with sustainable tourism, ensuring that future ple generations inherit the beauty and benefits of the natural world.

Our Mission & Values

Guided by principles of conservation, education, and community engagement.

Conservation

Protect and restore natural habitats, ensuring the survival of endangered species and the preservation of biodiversity.

Education

Inspire environmental awareness through guided tours, workshops, and interactive learning experiences for all ages.

Community

Engage with local communities to foster partnerships that support sustainable tourism and cultural heritage.
