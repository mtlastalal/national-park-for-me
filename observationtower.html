Observation Tower

The Observation Tower at GreenLeaf National Park offers a breathtaking 360-degree view of the park’s vast landscapes. Rising high above the treetops, it’s a favorite spot for birdwatchers, photographers, and sightseers. On clear days, you can see shimmering lakes, rolling meadows, and distant mountain ranges. The tower is accessible via a scenic trail that winds through lush forests, making the journey as rewarding as the destination. At sunrise and sunset, the sky transforms into a canvas of brilliant colors, making the tower a magical spot for unforgettable moments. Benches and telescopes at the top invite visitors to linger and soak in the panoramic beauty.
